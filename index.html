Natural enviroment: the Oiartzun Valley

Nestled in the heart of the Basque Country, the Oiartzun Valley offers a breathtaking blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Our horseback riding tours provide an unforgettable way to explore this picturesque region, located just a stone's throw from the French border.

As you ride through the valley, you'll be captivated by the stunning vistas that stretch from the rugged mountains to the shimmering sea. The lush, green landscape is a testament to the Basque people's deep connection to nature, a bond that has shaped their way of life for centuries. Our trails wind through scenic mountain paths, offering panoramic views that highlight the diverse beauty of the region. From the tranquil forests and rolling hills to the dramatic cliffs and coastal panoramas, every turn reveals a new and awe-inspiring sight. For the Basque people, being in touch with nature and ascending mountains is not just a pastime, but a vital part of their cultural identity. Our horseback riding experiences honor this tradition, providing a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the natural splendor that defines the Basque Country. Join us for a journey through the Oiartzun Valley, where every ride is a celebration of nature's beauty and the enduring spirit of the Basque culture. Whether you're an experienced rider or a beginner, our tours are designed to offer an enjoyable and enriching adventure for all. Come and discover why the Oiartzun Valley is a cherished gem of the Basque Country, and let the serene landscapes and cultural richness leave a lasting impression on your heart.

Basque Gastronomy

Experience Basque Gastronomy on Horseback: A Culinary Journey from Sea to Mountain Visitors to San Sebastian are often drawn by the city's renowned culinary scene, which boasts a vibrant and world-famous gastronomic reputation. Our horseback riding tours offer a unique opportunity to delve into the very roots of Basque cuisine, tracing its humble origins from traditional sea and mountain dishes that have inspired Michelin-starred chefs.

Our tour culminates in a visit to a traditional mountain inn, where you will savor a menu featuring classic Basque dishes. Each plate is crafted using the finest and freshest seasonal ingredients, embodying the simplicity and authenticity of the region's interior cuisine. Join us for an unforgettable journey through the Oiartzun Valley, where the natural beauty of the Basque Country meets its rich culinary heritage. Enjoy the flavors that have shaped Basque culture, all while experiencing the serene landscapes that make this area so special
